My Service

  • Service 1

    Volunteered as a coding mentor for middle and high school students, helping them learn the basics of Python, web development, and problem-solving. Guided students through hands-on projects, encouraged curiosity, and provided feedback to strengthen their understanding of computer science. This experience not only allowed me to share my passion for technology but also improved my ability to explain complex concepts in simple, engaging ways.

  • Service 2

    Assisted at a local food bank by organizing donations, preparing food packages, and distributing supplies to families in need. Worked collaboratively with volunteers to streamline the distribution process and ensure that over 200 families received meals weekly. This opportunity gave me insight into the importance of teamwork, service, and addressing community needs with efficiency and compassion.

  • Service 3

    Participated in campus-led environmental initiatives focused on sustainability and conservation. Activities included tree planting, recycling drives, and awareness campaigns encouraging students to reduce waste and make eco-friendly choices. Through these efforts, I contributed to creating a cleaner campus environment while also developing a deeper appreciation for the role small actions play in driving meaningful change.
